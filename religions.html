VIEWPOINTS OF DIFFERENT RELIGIONS

HOW DO OTHER RELIGIONS VIEW THE RELATIONSHIP BETWEEN SCIENCE AND RELIGION? DOES IT FOLLOW IAN BARBOUR'S 4 MODELS?

CHRISTIANITY

When Charles Darwin published his book Origin of Species and included Decent of Man, religious leaders expressed their critcism of Darwin's writing. Evolution, the advancement of species through natural selection stated that humans evolved from ape-like ancesters categorizing them as different species(homo sapiens, homo erectus, and neanderthals). By expanding on his observation and studies from the Voyage of the Beagle, Darwin introduced an idea contrary to what was known of human origins as stated in the Bible. This sparked intellectual discussion prompting scientists and theologians to discuss how this theory could be accepted in an Anglican community. If we look at today's society, many different sects of Christianity exist as they identify their various interpretations of the Bible. Conservative churches refuse to accept the theory of evolution stating that it goes against Biblical scripture. However, other sects such as non-denominational churches have expressed acceptance of particular scientific theories while still remaining faithful. Religious leaders such as Pope Francis have encouraged metaphorical analysis of the Bible in order to accept the positive impacts of scientific research.

ISLAM

The Quran teaches creation story of Adam and Eve similar to Hebrew text. Therefore, traditional societies are committed to interpreting sacred text as historical and scientific. Modern Muslim communities have taken steps to encourage metaphorical interpretation. Opinions include appreciating scientific reasoning in order to enhance human knowledge. If God has revealed the methods in which he created the world, we should be at awe of this and further gain faith in his power (compatible). "Do they not look at the sky above them? How We have built it and adorned it, and there are no rifts therein? And the earth We have spread it out, and set thereon mountains standing firm, and caused it to bring forth plants of beauteous kinds (in pairs). An insight and a Reminder for every slave who turns to God. And We send down from the sky blessed water whereby We give growth unto gardens and the grain of crops. And tall palm-trees, with shoots of fruit-stalks, piled one over another" (Qur'an 50:6-10),(Mansour, 284).
